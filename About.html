We are Japali Home Foods – A Taste of Tradition, A World of Flavour!

What began as a humble venture in 1979 under the name Vamana Pickles by Tejas Food Industries LLP, has today blossomed into an iconic brand, Swetha Telugu Foods. Rooted in the rich culinary traditions of South India, we have made it our mission to bring you the authentic, bold, and vibrant flavors that have made South Indian cuisine a global sensation.

Our journey, now spanning over four decades, has been guided by the true spirit of Indian home-cooked meals. From our signature pickles to masalas, snacks, powders, and pastes, every product we create is the best in taste.

Our Philosophy

At japali Home Foods , we believe in authenticity above all else. Our products are made with 100% natural ingredients and are preservative-free, ensuring that each bite is as pure and flavorful as it is healthy. We operate from a state-of-the-art, US FDA-approved facility near Hyderabad, adhering to stringent FSSAI and ISO 22000:2005 certifications, and following WHO-recommended safety protocols to guarantee the highest standards of hygiene and quality.

Global Reach

Today, japali Home Foods is a trusted name, not just in India, but across the world. With a presence in 28+ countries including Canada, the USA, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, UAE, Kuwait, and Hong Kong, we have made it our mission to bring the authentic taste of home to Indians everywhere. In India, we proudly serve the Southern states, preserving the regional flavors that define the heart of South Indian cuisine.

Our Promise

With over two decades of experience, our passionate team continually strives to create premium, handcrafted products that bring the best of traditional South Indian cooking to your table. Every product is rigorously tested for quality and taste, ensuring that only the finest reaches you.

Taste the Authentic Difference

When you choose japali home Foods, you’re choosing more than just food – you're choosing a slice of history, culture, and the genuine flavors of India. We are proud to be your partner in bringing the authentic home tastes of South India into kitchens around the world.
